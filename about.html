MY STORY

I started training seriously at 19 — not because I wanted to look good, but because I was tired of feeling weak. Weak in my body, weak in my discipline, weak in the way I showed up for the things that mattered. The gym became the one place where the rules were simple: put in the work, get the result.

"I don't believe in motivation. I believe in systems. Motivation fades. Systems are what remain when the excitement wears off."

After earning my NSCA-CSCS certification and spending six years training clients in-person, I made the switch to online coaching in 2020. Not because it was trendy — because I realized the best coaching doesn't require proximity. It requires precision, communication, and an obsessive attention to detail. Online coaching, done right, delivers all three.

"The clients I work with aren't beginners to hard work. They're beginners to having someone who refuses to let them settle."

Over 500 clients later, the APEX method has become something I'm genuinely proud of. Every program is built from the ground up — no templates, no recycled PDF plans. I study your movement, your lifestyle, your obstacles, and your psychology. Then I build something that doesn't just work for 12 weeks. It works for life. That's what separates coaching from content.
